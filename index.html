About The Studio

Art That Stays With You

Harsha Tattoo Studio is Coimbatore's premier destination for custom tattoo artistry, located within Shivalaiya Mahal. Here, skill meets creativity and hygiene, ensuring every design is executed with precision — from intricate fine-line details to bold, timeless traditional pieces. At Harsha, a tattoo isn't just ink; it's a permanent expression of your story, identity, and passion.

Founded by Akash Ganeshan, a visionary artist driven by deep passion and artistic excellence, the studio reflects his commitment to perfection. His work is known for its attention to detail, originality, and emotional depth — each tattoo he creates carries meaning beyond aesthetics. With a strong interest in evolving tattoo styles and a dedication to understanding every client's story, Akash transforms ideas into powerful, lasting art that speaks for a lifetime.
