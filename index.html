About The Studio

Art That Stays With You

Harsha Tattoo Studio is Coimbatore's premier destination for custom tattoo artistry. Healing Guide

Tattoo Aftercare

Taking proper care of your new tattoo is crucial to ensure it heals perfectly and preserves its details. Here are the essential Do's and Don'ts for the initial healing phase.

  • Leave Bandage On: Keep the original bandage or protective film on for 2–4 hours as advised by your artist.
  • Wash Gently: Wash the tattoo gently with warm water and a fragrance-free, mild antibacterial soap using your hands.
  • Pat Dry: Gently pat the tattoo dry with a clean paper towel. Do not rub or scrub the area.
  • Apply Ointment: Apply a very thin layer of recommended aftercare ointment or unscented lotion to keep it hydrated.
  • Let it Breathe: Wear clean, loose cotton clothing over the tattoo to prevent irritation and friction.
  • Don't Scratch or Pick: As it heals, the tattoo will peel and itch. Letting scabs fall off naturally is critical to avoid color loss.
  • Don't Submerge: Avoid swimming pools, hot tubs, ocean water, or long baths for at least 2 weeks. Showers are fine!
  • Don't Expose to Sun: Keep the tattoo out of direct sunlight during the healing process. Apply sunblock only after it's fully healed.
  • Don't Wear Tight Clothes: Avoid tight garments or straps that rub against the fresh ink, causing friction.
  • Don't Over-moisturize: Applying too much lotion can suffocate the skin and cause bubbles or delay healing.
